Welcome to my site. I am a Senior Software Developer at Immerse Learning. I love working with technology and my current job at Immerse is great. It gives me the opportunity to work with some cutting edge web tech, ranging from building a multiplayer websocket server in Go Lang to experimenting with WebGL and the latest Javascript ES6 spec.

My route into computer science was a fairly unconventional one. Having initially studied Architecture, which was a brilliant degree, my excitement with how technology was advancing the world enticed me into the world of computing.

Prior to working at Immerse I have run my own company which connected language learners and teachers, as well as working at a number of smaller startups. With every new project I take on I always try to satisfy my passion for learning by learning a new technology or language. This has given me experience with a variety of different technologies.

In my spare time if I am not programming a new game I will probably be taking part in a cycle race, or planning a kayaking adventure down the Yangtze!
